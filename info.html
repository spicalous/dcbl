TRAVEL INFORMATION

Parking is available on site.
Closest train station is Guildford.

Please note that a taxi MUST be booked before the event or no later then 8pm on the day.
A taxi called at midnight may take over an hour to arrive.
The approximate cost of a taxi to/from Gate Street Barn to Guildford station is £20 before 11pm and £30 after 11pm.
UBER is not available from the barn!
